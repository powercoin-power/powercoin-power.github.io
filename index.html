Powercoin - the power of world people's coin!

Powercoin is a new altcoin with both PoW/PoS implementation, and it's hoping to be world people's coin.

Summary

  • based on teslacoin, using scrypt algorithm
  • hybrid Proof of Stake / Proof of work
  • 61.3 reward in each mined block
  • 4 times special hourly reward (mining for over a hour)
  • 8 times special daily reward (mining for over a day)
  • 4 minutes blocks
  • zero premine - the developer was only started CPU mining today (on the day when it's released)
